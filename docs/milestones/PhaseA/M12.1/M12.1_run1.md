3. **Tests:** 175 failures persist — **critical finding**

### Unexpected Deltas

**Critical Finding:** `populate_by_name=True` does **not** work for dict inputs in Pydantic 2.0.3 when models have aliases defined.

**Evidence:**
- Local testing shows `populate_by_name=True` works for keyword arguments
- Local testing shows `populate_by_name=True` works for dict inputs when tested directly
- CI failures show models receiving dicts with snake_case keys, but Pydantic expects camelCase aliases
- Error pattern: `input_type=dict` with snake_case keys → Pydantic looks for camelCase aliases

**Hypothesis:** When Pydantic internally processes nested models or converts keyword arguments to dicts, it may not respect `populate_by_name=True` for dict validation when aliases are present.
